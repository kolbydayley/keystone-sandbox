# DEA Licensure Requirements for Nurse Practitioners

**Researched:** 2026-01-18
**Status:** Complete

## Overview

To prescribe controlled substances, Paige needs:
1. **Federal DEA Registration** — Required in each state where she practices
2. **State Controlled Substance License** — Some states require separate state CS license

## Federal DEA Registration

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Form | DEA Form 224 (New Application) |
| Fee | **$888 for 3 years** |
| Renewal | Every 3 years via Form 224a |
| Timeline | 4-6 weeks typically |
| Apply | [DEA Diversion Control](https://apps.deadiversion.usdoj.gov/webforms2/spring/main?execution=e1s1) |

### Requirements
- Valid state NP license with prescriptive authority
- State controlled substance license (if required)
- Physical practice address in the state
- One registration needed per state where prescribing

### Process
1. Complete online Form 224
2. Pay $888 fee
3. Wait for processing (4-6 weeks)
4. Receive DEA number

### MATE Act Training (Required)
As of June 2023, DEA requires **8 hours of training** on treatment and management of opioid/substance use disorders before first DEA registration or renewal.

## Utah State Requirements

### Controlled Substance License
Utah requires a **separate state CS license** from DOPL in addition to DEA.

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Agency | Utah DOPL (Division of Professional Licensing) |
| Apply | [dopl.utah.gov/nursing](https://dopl.utah.gov/nursing/apply-for-a-license/controlled-substance/) |
| Prerequisite | Active Utah APRN license |

### Continuing Education for CS
- **4 hours** of controlled substance prescribing CE per renewal period
  - 3.5 hours of approved CS education
  - 0.5 hours DOPL online CS tutorial

## Massachusetts State Requirements

### Controlled Substance Registration
MA requires registration with:
- **MA DPH** (Department of Public Health) for prescriptive authority
- **MA PMP** (Prescription Monitoring Program) registration

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Agency | MA Board of Registration in Nursing |
| PMP | [mass.gov/orgs/prescription-monitoring-program](https://www.mass.gov/orgs/prescription-monitoring-program) |
| Requirement | Must check PMP before prescribing Schedule II-V |

## Summary: What Paige Needs

### For Utah Practice
1. ✅ Utah APRN license (verify status)
2. ⬜ Utah Controlled Substance license from DOPL
3. ⬜ DEA Registration for Utah address ($888)
4. ⬜ MATE Act training (8 hours) if not already completed

### For Massachusetts Practice
1. ⬜ MA APRN license (verify status / apply if needed)
2. ⬜ MA prescriptive authority
3. ⬜ MA PMP registration
4. ⬜ DEA Registration for MA address ($888)
5. ⬜ MATE Act training (same training counts for both)

### Total DEA Cost
- **$1,776** ($888 × 2 states) for 3 years
