# ERP / Practice Management Comparison

**Researched:** 2026-01-18
**Status:** Complete

## Overview

Practice management software for psychiatric NP private practice. Key needs:
- Telehealth capabilities
- Insurance billing/claims
- Scheduling
- EHR/documentation
- E-prescribing (important for psych)
- Multi-state support

---

## Options Compared

### SimplePractice ⭐ Top Pick for Solo Psych Practice

**Pricing (as of 2025):**
| Plan | Monthly | Features |
|------|---------|----------|
| Starter | ~$29 | Basic EHR, scheduling |
| Essential | ~$69 | + Telehealth, billing |
| Plus | ~$99 | + Advanced features, reports |

**Pros:**
- Most popular for mental health practitioners
- Excellent telehealth built-in
- Strong insurance billing features
- AI-driven documentation (new)
- Good client portal
- E-prescribing available (add-on ~$40/mo)

**Cons:**
- Add-ons can increase cost
- Some users report billing complexity
- Pricing changed in 2025 (some features moved to higher tiers)

**Best for:** Solo practitioners, mental health focus

---

### Jane App

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Monthly | Features |
|------|---------|----------|
| Practice | $54 | Core features, 1 practitioner |
| Thrive | $79 | + Advanced features |
| Insurance Add-on | +$20 | Insurance billing |

**Pros:**
- Clean, intuitive interface
- Transparent pricing
- Regular updates/new features
- Good for multi-provider clinics
- Strong scheduling

**Cons:**
- Insurance billing is add-on (+$20/mo)
- Less mental health-specific features
- Originally built for physical health

**Best for:** Clinics with multiple providers, those who prefer cleaner interface

---

### TherapyNotes

**Pricing:** ~$49-69/month (varies by features)

**Pros:**
- Built specifically for behavioral health
- Strong documentation templates for therapy
- Reliable customer support
- Good telehealth

**Cons:**
- Interface feels dated to some
- Billing can be complex
- Less modern than competitors

**Best for:** Traditional therapy practices, those who want behavioral health-specific

---

### Headway (Alternative Model)

**Not traditional ERP** — Headway handles credentialing and billing for you.

**Pricing:** They take a cut of insurance payments

**Pros:**
- Handles insurance credentialing
- Manages billing/claims
- Can help build patient panel

**Cons:**
- Give up some revenue
- Less control
- Not a full ERP solution

**Best for:** Those who don't want to deal with insurance billing themselves

## Recommendation

For **psychiatric NP private practice**, **SimplePractice** is the best fit:

1. **Mental health focus** — Built for this use case
2. **E-prescribing option** — Critical for psych
3. **Strong telehealth** — Important for multi-state practice
4. **Insurance billing included** — In mid-tier plan
5. **Large user community** — Easy to get help

**Suggested plan:** Essential ($69/mo) + E-prescribe add-on ($40/mo) = **~$109/month**

### Second Choice: Jane App
If SimplePractice pricing is too high or interface doesn't fit, Jane App at $74/month (Practice + Insurance) is solid alternative.
